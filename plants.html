Plant Gallery

A small collection of popular houseplants with quick care tips.

Monstera (Monstera deliciosa)

Intermediate

Big dramatic leaves, likes bright indirect light and occasional watering. Support with a moss pole for climbing.

Bright Indirect Water: Weekly-ish

Snake Plant (Sansevieria)

Easy

Tolerant of low light and drought — perfect for beginners and offices. Let soil dry out between waterings.

Low Maintenance Water: Every 2–6 weeks

ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia)

Easy

Glossy leaves, extremely drought-tolerant and survives in low-light spots. Avoid overwatering.

Tough Water: Rarely

Fiddle Leaf Fig (Ficus lyrata)

Advanced

Needs bright, consistent light and regular watering. Prone to leaf drop if conditions change suddenly.

Bright Light Water: Weekly

Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

Very Easy

Fast-growing trailing plant that tolerates many light conditions. Great for hanging baskets or shelves.

Beginner Water: Every 1–2 weeks
