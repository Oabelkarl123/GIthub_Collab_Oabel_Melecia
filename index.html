What are Prairies?

Prairies are large, open ecosystems dominated by grasses, herbs, and shrubs, with few trees, found in temperate grassland biomes.

National Geographic Biomes

What are Plains?

Plains are flat, and open areas of land with few changes in elevation. They can be formed by erosion, volcanic activity, or river sediments.
