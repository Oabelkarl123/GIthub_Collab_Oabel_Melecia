What is Prairies?

Prairies are large, open ecosystems dominated by grasses, herbs, and shrubs, with few trees, found in temperate grassland biomes.

National Geographic Biomes

What is Plains?

Plains is a flat, and open area of land with few changes in elevation, and they are found on every continent. These flatlands can be formed by erosion, volcanic activity, or the deposition of sediment by rivers and can support diverse ecosystems like grasslands, savannas, and forests.
